How to navigate in the Universe

Zooming: Use the zoom buttons to zoom in and out of the solar system.

Moving the View: Use right-click + drag to move the canvas and explore the solar system.

Speed: Use the speed slider to adjust the speed at which the planets orbit the Sun. The faster the speed, the quicker the planets move.
